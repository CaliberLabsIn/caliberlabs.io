Frequently Asked Questions

Do you offer contract research services?

Yes, we offer R&D services in manufacturing systems development, materials characterization, and prototyping. Contact us to discuss your project requirements.

Are you open to academic collaborations?

Absolutely! We actively collaborate with universities and research institutions. We're interested in joint projects, student internships, and knowledge exchange.

Can I visit your facility?

We welcome visits from potential collaborators, investors, and partners. Please contact us in advance to schedule a visit to our Chikhli facility.

How can I apply for a job?

Check our Careers page for current openings. You can also send your resume to careers@caliberlab.in for general consideration.

What areas do you specialize in?

We focus on regenerative medicine (biomaterials, tissue engineering) and advanced manufacturing (3D printing, bio-printers, water cutting, embedded systems). See our Research and Capabilities pages for details.

Do you work with startups or industry partners?

Yes. We partner with startups and companies for custom R&D, prototyping, and technology development. Reach out to discuss your goals and how we can support your project.
