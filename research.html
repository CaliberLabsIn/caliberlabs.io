Regenerative Medicine Research

Our regenerative medicine division focuses on developing next-generation biomaterials and tissue engineering solutions for oral healthcare and beyond. We're pioneering affordable, effective treatments for bone and soft tissue regeneration.

Research Focus Areas

Bone Regeneration Materials

Developing bioactive scaffolds and growth factor delivery systems for bone defect repair.

Soft Tissue Engineering

Creating biomimetic materials for gum and periodontal tissue regeneration.

Advanced Biomaterials

Synthesizing novel biocompatible polymers and ceramics with enhanced properties.

Clinical Translation

Working with dental and medical professionals to validate and refine our solutions.

Current Projects

  • Bioactive glass scaffolds for alveolar bone regeneration
  • Injectable hydrogels for minimally invasive tissue repair
  • 3D-printed patient-specific implants
  • Growth factor encapsulation technologies

Advanced Manufacturing Research

Our manufacturing division is revolutionizing accessible 3D printing technology. We're developing low-cost, high-precision systems that democratize advanced manufacturing for research labs, startups, and educational institutions.

Research Focus Areas

Bio 3D Printers

Custom extrusion systems for bioink and biomaterial deposition with precise temperature and pressure control.

FDM Systems

High-precision filament printers with advanced motion control and multi-material capabilities.

SLA Technology

Resin-based stereolithography for high-resolution prototyping and end-use parts.

Water Cutting

Precision abrasive waterjet systems for cutting complex geometries in various materials.

Technical Capabilities

  • Custom embedded control systems (STM32, ESP32)
  • Real-time motion planning algorithms
  • Precision thermal management
  • Multi-axis CNC integration
  • IoT-enabled monitoring and control
